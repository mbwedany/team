Reading is life

Why do we like to read?

The more we read, the more imaginative we become. Whenever we read a fiction book, it takes us into another world. In the new world, our imagination works at its best as we try to see things in our own mind.

Other reasons to start reading today:
  • Develops critical thinking skills
  • Builds vocabulary
  • Improves writing skills
  • Improves communication skills
